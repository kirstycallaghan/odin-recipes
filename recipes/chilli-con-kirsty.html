Chilli Con Kirsty

Try this delicious spin on chilli con carne with my magic ingredient - Baked Beans!

  • 500g Beef Mince
  • 1 tin of Baked Beans
  • 1 tin of Kidney Beans
  • 1 tin of Black Beans
  • 2 tins of chopped tomatoes (REALLY recommend you try the Mutti brand tomatoes, they are expensive but so good!)
  • 1 Courguette chopped into chunks
  • 1 Onion diced
  • 1 Carrot chopped into chunks
  • 1 Pack of Hot Chilli Con Carne seasoning mix (We like Sainsburys own brand)
  1. Chuck EVERYTHING into the slow cooker
  2. Take a wooden spoon and mix it all together
  3. Set the timer at low for 6 hours or high for 4 hours
  4. Stir once an hour or so (unless you're out at work then don't worry about it!)
